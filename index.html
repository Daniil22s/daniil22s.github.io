About

I was born and raised in Vladivostok, graduated in 2022 from Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology (MIPT). Currently, I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at Johns Hopkins University (JHU). I work in Birational Geometry under the supervision of Professor Shokurov. My research is related to boundedness questions for Calabi-Yau varieties.
